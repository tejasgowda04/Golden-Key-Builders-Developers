Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about BMRDA approved properties and our services

What does BMRDA approval mean for my property?

BMRDA (Bangalore Metropolitan Region Development Authority) approval means the property is legally sanctioned for development, has proper infrastructure planning, and ensures future appreciation. It provides legal security and makes the property bankable for loans.

What is A Khata and why is it important?

A Khata is a document that proves you are the legal owner of a property and have paid all taxes. It's essential for obtaining bank loans, building permits, and selling the property in the future. All our properties come with clear A Khata documentation.

Do you assist with home loans?

Yes, we have tie-ups with major banks and financial institutions. Our BMRDA approved A Khata properties are easily eligible for home loans. We assist throughout the loan application and approval process.

Can NRIs invest in your properties?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience working with NRI clients. We handle all legal formalities, provide regular updates, and ensure smooth transactions with complete transparency. Our properties are FEMA compliant for NRI investments.
